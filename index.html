Refund Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

At Vertex Home Solutions, we stand behind the quality of our leads. Our refund and replacement policy is designed to be fair, transparent, and easy to use.

Lead Replacement Guarantee

We offer a lead replacement guarantee on all plans. If a lead meets any of the following criteria, we will replace it at no charge:

  • Wrong or disconnected phone number
  • Lead is clearly outside your specified service area
  • Lead requested a service you do not offer
  • Duplicate lead already delivered within the same billing cycle

How to Request a Replacement

To request a lead replacement, contact us within 48 hours of receiving the lead via email at vertexhomesolution@gmail.com. Include the lead's name, phone number, and the reason for the replacement request. We process replacements within 1–2 business days.

What Is Not Covered

  • Leads where the homeowner was contacted but did not proceed — this is a sales outcome, not a lead quality issue
  • Leads where no contact attempt was made within 24 hours
  • Leads disputed more than 48 hours after delivery

Monetary Refunds

Monetary refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. If you are unsatisfied with your first month of service and lead quality was consistently below our stated standards, contact us and we will work with you on a fair resolution, which may include a partial or full refund at our discretion.

No Refunds On

  • Partial months of service
  • Leads that met our quality standards but did not convert
  • Cancellations made mid-cycle

Contact

For any refund or replacement requests, reach us at vertexhomesolution@gmail.com. We respond within 24 hours on business days.
